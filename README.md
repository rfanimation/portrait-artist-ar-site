## The Problem Every Artist Knows

You glance at your reference. Look at your canvas. Back to the reference. Back to the canvas. Is that eye too high? Is the shadow shape right? After hundreds of these micro-comparisons, your concentration is shattered and you're still not sure if your proportions are accurate.

## The Solution

Portrait Artist AR uses your iPhone's LiDAR sensor to project your reference image directly onto your physical canvas in augmented reality. No more guessing. No more endless back-and-forth. Just overlay, verify, and draw with confidence.

---

## How It Works

**1. Load Your Reference**
Select any image from your photo library â€” a photograph, master study, or your own sketch.

**2. Anchor to Your Canvas**
Point your camera at the marker near your canvas. Tap to lock the overlay in 3D space.

**3. Adjust & Align**
Scale, rotate, and position until your reference perfectly matches your canvas dimensions.

**4. Draw with Confidence**
Slide the opacity to check your work anytime. The overlay stays locked while you create.
